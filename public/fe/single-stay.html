  Description

Ubud Permai Bungalow & Spa is located in the Downtown Ubud neighborhood in Ubud, just 501 m from Bebek Bengil Restaurant.

Certain rooms have a sitting area where you can relax. Certain rooms include views of the pool or garden. Each room has a private bathroom.

You will find ticket service at the property.

The guesthouse also provides bike rental. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International Airport, 27.4 km from Ubud Permai Bungalow.

This is our guests' favorite part of Ubud, according to independent reviews.
